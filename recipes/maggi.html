Maggi Noodles

So this is the most basic recipe i know, it is instant noodles that i loved to eat.
so lets get started:
to make this recipe we need those items:

    Ingredients

  • Maggi(Obviously)
  • Green Peas
  • Coriander leaves
  • Water
  • 2 minutes (or maybe more)

    Steps

  1. Take 2 cups of water and boil it
  2. Break maggi in half
  3. put maggi in boiling water
  4. Add the maagi masala in it
  5. Let the water waporized
  6. add Green peas and coriander leaves
  7. Enjoy!!!!
